About Us

ROMetrics is designed for students, clinicians, and anyone pursuing an accurate understanding of human movement—particularly those interested in physical therapy, kinesiology, and exercise science.

Purpose

Developed to provide a clear, interactive space for exploring the complexities of human movement.

Anatomical Foundation

By utilizing anatomically aligned 3D models and joint constraints, we ensure that every measurement and observation reflects the nuances of human anatomy.

Mechanical Visualization

Helps users visualize the mechanical relationships behind movement outcomes, fostering a deeper understanding of balance, strength, and range of motion.

Educational Integration

Serves as a bridge between foundational learning and clinical application. Specialized areas like the Forensic Motion Lab support advanced coursework and case-based analysis.

Professional Confidence

Prioritizes clinical reasoning and evidence-based principles to help users build the precise assessment skills required for professional practice.
